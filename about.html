About Us

Charity & Hope Africa is dedicated to empowering women across Africa through education, business support, and community programs. We believe in equality, growth, and sustainable impact.

Our Mission

To provide women with the tools, resources, and network to succeed in their communities and business ventures.

Our Vision

Africa where women are empowered, educated, and thriving in every sector of society.
